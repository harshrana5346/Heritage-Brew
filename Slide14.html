Quick Insight

Youth preferences are shifting toward craft, organic, low-alcohol and non-alcoholic beverages. Sustainability and natural ingredients are primary purchase drivers.
70% of urban millennials prefer beverages with natural/functional ingredients. Low/no added sugar options are gaining share — Varun Beverages reported 55% sales from this category in H1 2025.
